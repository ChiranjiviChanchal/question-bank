Introduction

Marwari College,is founded in 1941,is a constituent unit (N.A.A.C. Accredited 'A' Grade) of T.M. Bhagalpur University. It occupies the foremost position among the peer colleges of the university and is acclaimed as an institution for imparting quality education in the faculties of Science, Arts and Vocational courses. Currently, this College Principal is  Sri K.C.Jha
